Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose Sell2Rent?
The majority of our properties are off-market and have never been listed online. Thanks to our sale-leaseback program, most of our properties also include a homeowner willing to rent from day one.
Who is the seller?
The sellers are homeowners who have contacted us for the Sell & Leaseback program. Many want to tap into their home equity and are willing to stay as renters after a sale.
How long does the buying process take?
The usual process takes about 30-45 days to complete.
What underwriting is done by Sell2Rent?
Preliminary underwriting is done by us as we analyze: (1) The Seller, (2) The Property, and (3) The Financial Transaction.
What types of properties can I invest in?
We focus exclusively on single-family homes and townhomes that are occupied by the homeowner.
What types of properties do you work with?
We focus exclusively on single-family homes and townhomes that are occupied by the homeowner.
What areas do you serve?
We work across the US.
Who controls the closing?
In most cases, Sell2rent will arrange the closing using institutional-quality service providers.
As an investor, how much does it cost to use Sell2Rent?
Nothing. The investor does not pay to use our platform.
What is the typical leaseback period for a seller?
Rent agreements typically range from 1 to 3 years. Many sellers are willing to pay rent several months in advance. Ultimately, the terms of the lease are negotiated and agreed to by both your client and the buyer.
What if I don't want to rent the house to the former owner?
We also have vacant properties you can invest in!
