About Us

Feeling House Rich and Cash Poor?

You’re not alone. And while interest rates get higher and loan restrictions stronger, you’re left with fewer options to access your home equity.

But unlike loans and reverse mortgages, our sale-leaseback program can deliver on your equity in less time and with less effort.

What’s the Catch?

There’s no catch. We find investors in our network who want to buy your home and they’re happy to let you rent it back. You stay home with cash equity and life gets easier while you rent.

Sell & Leaseback Program

How it Works

  • 1
    Send Us Your Information

    Click “Get Started” to begin. You can also “Schedule a Meeting” with one of our agents or call 1-800-954-6373.
  • 2
    We Match You With Home-Buying Investors

    We don’t buy or lend. Instead, we find buyers in our national network and help you get offers so you can sell and stay.

  • 3
    Get a Cash Offer and Rental Terms

    The offers for your home will be as close to fair market value as possible. We’ll help you customize terms and negotiate your rental agreement.
  • 4
    Time for a Property Inspection

    If you accept the terms of the sale and rental agreement, we then schedule a detailed inspection to verify the home’s condition.
  • 5
    Close, Cash-Out and Enjoy Your Home

    On the day of closing, we’ll have a final walkthrough. You then finish closing, get paid and live happily in the same home.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why work with Sell2Rent?
Many of our sellers have specific requirements for timing when buying & selling or have financial needs that are best met by a sale-leaseback. Thanks to our program and our investor network, we can meet these needs more quickly than a traditional sale would.
Who is buying these properties?
Our core buyers are investors looking to acquire single-family homes as investments. We partner with all types of investors, from local individuals to funds and institutions. Bids and negotiations are managed by each investor, with Sell2Rent in a facilitating role.
What if I don't want to rent the house to the former owner?
We also have vacant properties you can invest in!
Who is buying the property?
TOur core buyers are investors looking to acquire SFH’s as an investment (often generating cashflow day ONE). We partner with all types of investors, from individual investors to funds/institutions. These investors are the ones who ultimately purchase the property, whereas S2R operates as the platform that connects them with the sellers..
