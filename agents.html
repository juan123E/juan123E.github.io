The Process

How it works

  • Submit your listing and property specifications.

  • We’ll match your client with investors in our network.

  • Receive cash offers and rental terms for review with your client.

  • After closing, your client gets cash equity and stays as a renter, while you get your regular commission.

Working With Sell2Rent Means

We Want What’s Best for The Homeowner

Since we don’t buy homes ourselves, we hope to foster competitive bidding between buyers to get the best offers for homeowner clients. We facilitate progress (with you) along the way and aim to give the seller good options for moving or staying.

You Remain in Control of Your Deals

While we work behind the scenes, you’ll be the main point of contact for your client, the seller, as is standard. We’ll communicate with the buyer. 

You’ll Get Access to Top Investors in The Market

Frequently Asked Questions

Why work with Sell2Rent?
Many of our sellers have specific requirements for timing when buying & selling or have financial needs that are best met by a sale-leaseback. Thanks to our program and our investor network, we can meet these needs more quickly than a traditional sale would.
Who is buying these properties?
Our core buyers are investors looking to acquire single-family homes as investments. We partner with all types of investors, from local individuals to funds and institutions. Bids and negotiations are managed by each investor, with Sell2Rent in a facilitating role.
How long does the buying process take?
The process typically takes about 30-45 days.
What underwriting is done by Sell2Rent?
Preliminary underwriting is done by us as we analyze: (1) The Seller, (2) The Property, and (3) The Financial Transaction.
What types of properties do you work with?
We focus exclusively on single-family homes and townhomes that are occupied by the homeowner.
What areas do you serve?
We work across the US.
Who controls the closing?
In most cases, Sell2rent will arrange the closing using institutional-quality service providers.
As an agent, how much does it cost to use Sell2Rent?
Nothing. The agent does not pay to use our platform.
What is the typical leaseback period for a seller?
Rent agreements typically range from 1 to 3 years. Many sellers are willing to pay rent several months in advance. Ultimately, the terms of the lease are negotiated and agreed to by both your client and the buyer.
